Thanks for checking out the readme. Hatty's Tools is going to be a website that allows anyone to use the tools I create. This is in its very early stages. Today March/19/2026 we have a couple things working.

Crypto Profit / Loss Calculator
Enter your entry price, exit price, and position size to see your actual gain or loss, fees included. Results are color-coded for instant clarity.

DCA Planner
Type a ticker — the current price auto-fills from CoinGecko. Model dollar-cost averaging with real historical prices and see your average cost and total stack.

"Can I Make It?" Simulator
Type a ticker — the current price auto-fills. Pick a target price and find out exactly what your position would be worth and whether you hit your goal.

I will be adding more as time goes on. Check out my other website to see what else I am building
